      • Phone Installation:

        • ●Tap/click: G-chan.app
          link (to copy)
        • ●Open browser homepage: Hold (🏠) located on top left corner
        • ●On pop-up: select Edit Homepage
        • ●Paste the link

        Desktop Installation:

        • ●For chrome-based Browsers eg: Brave, Edge or Chrome:
        • ●Due to age restrictions, sign in to your google account first before you visit the Chrome Webstore Link Below
        • ●Visit: G-chan Extension
        • ●Install Extension

      • Phone:

        • ●Open browser homepage: Hold (🏠) located on top left corner
        • ●On pop-up: select Edit Homepage
        • ●Select Default Homepage

        Desktop:

        • ●Open settings(☰) on the top-right corner:
        • ●Select: Extensions
        • ●Disable or Remove Extension
